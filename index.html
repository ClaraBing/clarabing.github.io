I am a master student at Stanford majoring in computer science. I work in the Stanford Vision and Learning Lab under the supervision of Li Fei-Fei and Juan Carlos Niebles. My research interest lies on video understanding. I am also passionate about applying computer vision techniques to improve healthcare, and I am working on the ICU team at Partnership in AI-Assisted Care (PAC).
